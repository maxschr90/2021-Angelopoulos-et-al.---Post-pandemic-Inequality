This folder contains 4 xlsx files.

	1. Data_Moments.xlsx contains the means and variances of the health measure. Out of these the conditional means
	   of healthy, sick and recovering households and the overall variance provide the targets for our calibration.
	2. ExogenousProcesses.xlsx contains the exogenous processes that enter the calibration of the model.
	3. Healthshockresponse.xlsx contains the responses to a severe health event which are used to construct the figures in appendix D.
	4. Tables.xlsx contains the information to construct tables 1, 2 and part of 4 in the main text and table 2A in the appendix.
